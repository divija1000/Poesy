Life isn't consistent,
Neither the time nor the people stay, but still, we live.
Sometimes we live in the good times, the rest we live hoping for one.
We meet new people, surround us with a new environment,
get involved in new activities, build new memories
And then a time comes for it to get over, and it does.
And what remains is memories.

Sometimes the cruelty of life is immeasurable,
And the pain of existence becomes intolerable
You are crying and screaming aloud, but nobody is there to listen

And you then realize the saddest reality of life,
That people only stay for the good times,
the bad you have to walk through alone.....

Life is not as easy as we imagine,
